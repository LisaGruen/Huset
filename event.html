FORVENTNINGEN OM VITA

A LOVE STORY OF LOSING
buy tickets
When Camilla and René lose their first child, they choose to take the child home - a decision their loved ones find difficult to accept. But what is 'the right grieving process'? Do you have to sing children's songs while choosing a tombstone? Do you have to take a walk with the child in the stroller? And how does one tell the outside world that one wants to do all these natural parent-ing with a child who did not come alive to the world? Every year in Denmark, around a thousand parent couples experience losing a child during pregnancy or around childbirth. In 'The Expectation of the Vita', we follow two of these parents who are going through a grief that their out-side world has difficulty understanding.
